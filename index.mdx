**How it works:** Describe your site → AI builds it → refine with the editor → publish.

## Quick Start

1. **Sign up** at [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register) with email or Google.
2. **Create a site** — use a [prompt](https://app.getsite.ai) or the [AI chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat).
3. **Edit** with the AI editor, then **publish**.
