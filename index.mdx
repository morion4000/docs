## What is GetSite?

GetSite.ai is an AI-powered website builder that turns a text prompt into a fully designed, hosted, and editable one-page website in under 60 seconds. No coding or design skills required.

## How It Works

1. **Describe** — Tell the AI about your business, your style, and your goals in plain language.
2. **Generate** — The AI builds a complete website with custom copy, images, and a modern responsive layout.
3. **Refine** — Use the AI editor to perfect every detail, then publish with one click.
