## What is GetSite?

GetSite is a comprehensive website creation and management platform that allows users to easily build, customize, and maintain websites. The [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai) application provides a user-friendly interface for website creation, domain management, asset handling, and various other web development tools.

## Getting Started

Ready to dive in? Here's how to get started with GetSite:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Register with your email address or use Google Sign-in at
    [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Confirm your email address (not required if using Google login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Navigate to your [personalized dashboard](https://app.getsite.ai/dashboard)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Website">
    Follow the guided process to [create your first
    site](https://app.getsite.ai)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plans and Pricing

GetSite offers multiple pricing tiers to accommodate different user needs:

- **Free Plan**: $0/month - Limited access to basic features
- **Maker Plan**: $10/month - 100 monthly credits, ideal for individual creators
- **Developer Plan**: $40/month - 500 monthly credits, perfect for professional developers
- **Agency Plan**: $160/month - 2,500 monthly credits, designed for agencies

Learn more about our [pricing plans](/pricing/plans) and [credits system](/pricing/credits).
