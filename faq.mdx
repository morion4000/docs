<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is GetSite.ai?">
    An AI website builder that creates a fully designed, hosted website from a text prompt in under 60 seconds. No coding or design skills needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
    Free tier with 1 site. Paid plans: Maker ($10/mo), Developer ($40/mo), Max ($160/mo). Yearly billing saves 30%. [Full pricing →](/pricing/plans)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. Maker plan on monthly billing includes a 5-day free trial with 30 bonus credits. Cancel before it ends to avoid charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are credits?">
    Credits power AI actions — creating sites (20), editing (20), generating images (1–2), etc. Each plan includes monthly credits. You can buy more separately. They reset each billing cycle and don't roll over. Purchased add-on credits never expire.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Yes. Buy, transfer, or link a domain. Maker: 10 domains, Developer: 50, Max: unlimited. Free subdomains on getsite.online and getsite.co are always available.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need hosting?">
    No. All paid plans include managed hosting with CDN and free SSL.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my site?">
    Yes. Download as ZIP with all HTML, CSS, and JS. Available on Maker plan and above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it mobile-friendly?">
    Yes. All generated sites are responsive by default.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add custom code?">
    Yes. Inject custom HTML, CSS, or JavaScript via the Scripts tab in site settings. Full code editor available on Developer plan and above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Access continues until the end of the billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do upgrades and downgrades work?">
    Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
