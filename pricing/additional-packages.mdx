### How to Download

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Website">
    From your dashboard, select the website you want to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Download">
    Look for the "Download" option in the website settings or version history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase">
    Complete the one-time payment if required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Files">
    Your website files will be prepared as a ZIP for download.
  </Step>
</Steps>
