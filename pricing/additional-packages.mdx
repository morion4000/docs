## Overview

Beyond the standard subscription plans, GetSite offers additional one-time purchase packages to enhance your experience. These packages provide extra functionality and resources without requiring a plan upgrade, giving you flexibility to add only what you need.

## Credit Packages

If you need more credits than your monthly allocation, you can purchase additional credit packages:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Small Package" icon="coins">
    100 credits for $20
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium Package" icon="coins">
    500 credits for $90
  </Card>
  <Card title="Large Package" icon="coins">
    1,000 credits for $160
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Credit Package Features

These additional credit packages offer several benefits:

- **One-time Purchase**: Not subscription-based, just pay once
- **No Expiration**: Credits never expire
- **Stackable**: Add to your monthly credit allocation
- **Immediate Availability**: Use right after purchase
- **All Features**: Can be used for any credit-consuming feature

### Purchasing Credit Packages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/account](https://app.getsite.ai/account).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Credits Section">
    Find the "Credits" section in your account dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Package">
    Choose the credit package that best fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase">
    Follow the payment process to complete your purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using Credits">
    Your additional credits will be immediately available for use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Feature Packages

### Download Website Package

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Price" icon="money-bill-wave">
    $10 per website
  </Card>
  <Card title="Type" icon="shopping-bag">
    One-time purchase
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The Download Website Package allows you to export and download your website files for use outside the GetSite platform:

- **Complete Export**: Get all HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and assets
- **Self-hosting**: Use the files on any hosting service
- **Ownership**: Full ownership of generated code
- **No Limitations**: No GetSite branding or restrictions
- **One-time Fee**: Pay once per website

<Card title="Developer & Agency Plans" icon="info-circle" color="#7b5dd6">
  The Download Website Package is included at no extra cost for users on the
  Developer and Agency plans.
</Card>

#### How to Purchase and Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Website">
    From your dashboard, select the website you want to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export Option">
    Look for the "Export" or "Download" option in the website settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase Package">
    If you're on the Free or Maker plan, you'll be prompted to purchase the
    download package.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Follow the payment process to complete your purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Files">
    Once payment is complete, your website files will be prepared for download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## White Label Packages

White Label packages allow agencies and professionals to rebrand the GetSite platform with their own branding:

### Basic White Label

- **Custom Logo**: Replace GetSite logo with your own
- **Brand Colors**: Apply your brand colors to the interface
- **Custom Domain**: Host the interface on your own domain
- **Hidden GetSite References**: Remove mentions of GetSite

### Premium White Label

All Basic features, plus:

- **Custom Email Templates**: Brand all email notifications
- **Client Portal**: Branded client access portal
- **Custom Welcome Screens**: Customize onboarding experience
- **Priority Support**: Dedicated white label support
- **API Customization**: Custom API endpoints

## Best Practices

- **Evaluate Needs**: Purchase additional packages only when they provide clear value
- **Credit Planning**: Monitor credit usage before purchasing additional packages
- **Domain Strategy**: Consider purchasing multiple variations of your domain for brand protection
- **White Label ROI**: Calculate the return on investment for white label packages based on client perception
- **Export Timing**: Purchase the Download Website Package when you need to migrate or create backups
