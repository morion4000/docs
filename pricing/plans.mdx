## Billing and Payment

### Billing Cycles

- **Monthly Billing**: Pay month-to-month with the flexibility to cancel anytime
- **Annual Billing**: Pay for a full year upfront and save 30% (equivalent to 4 months free)

### Payment Methods

GetSite accepts the following payment methods:

- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (for annual Agency plans only)

### Subscription Management

Manage your subscription easily through your account:

1. Log in to your GetSite account
2. Navigate to [Account Settings](https://app.getsite.ai/account)
3. Select the "Subscription" tab
4. From there, you can:
   - Upgrade or downgrade your plan
   - Switch between monthly and annual billing
   - Update payment information
   - View billing history
