## Available Plans

GetSite offers multiple pricing tiers. Yearly billing saves 30% (4 months free). For the most current pricing, visit the [official pricing page](https://www.getsite.ai/pricing).

### Plans Overview

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly | Credits/month |
|------|---------|--------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 0 |
| **Maker** | $10/mo | $84/yr ($7/mo) | 100 |
| **Developer** | $40/mo | $336/yr ($28/mo) | 500 |
| **Max** | $160/mo | $1,344/yr ($112/mo) | 2,500 |

The Maker plan includes a 5-day free trial on monthly billing with 30 bonus credits. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.

## Billing and Payment

### Billing Cycles

- **Monthly Billing**: Pay month-to-month with the flexibility to cancel anytime
- **Annual Billing**: Pay for a full year upfront and save 30% (equivalent to 4 months free)

### Upgrades and Downgrades

- **Upgrades** are prorated immediately.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the next billing cycle.
- **Cancellation**: Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
