## Overview

GetSite's Account Management feature provides a comprehensive interface for managing your user profile, preferences, subscription plans, and account settings. This central hub ensures that you can easily control all aspects of your GetSite experience from one location.

## Key Features

### Registration and Authentication

Create and access your GetSite account:

- **Account Creation**: Simple registration process
- **Email/Password Authentication**: Traditional login method
- **Google Authentication**: Quick sign-up and login with your Google account
- **Password Management**: Reset and update your password
- **Security Features**: Two-factor authentication and security settings

### User Profile Management

Manage your personal information and preferences:

- **Profile Information**: Update your name, email, and other details
- **Profile Picture**: Add or change your profile image
- **Notification Preferences**: Control what notifications you receive
- **Language Settings**: Choose your preferred language
- **Time Zone Settings**: Set your local time zone

### Subscription Management

Control your GetSite subscription:

- **Plan Selection**: Choose and update your subscription plan
- **Payment Information**: Manage payment methods
- **Billing History**: Access past invoices and billing statements
- **Plan Comparison**: Compare features across different plans
- **Upgrade/Downgrade**: Change your subscription level

### Credits Management

Monitor and manage your credits:

- **Credits Balance**: View your current credits balance
- **Credits Usage History**: Track how credits have been used
- **Purchase Additional Credits**: Buy more credits as needed
- **Usage Estimates**: See estimates for planned actions
- **Auto-replenish Settings**: Configure automatic credit purchases

### Account Deletion

Option to delete your account.

## Usage Instructions

### Creating an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration Page">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Registration Method">
    Select either email/password registration or Google authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Information">
    Provide the required information (email, password).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    If using email registration, verify your email address through the sent
    link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Optionally complete additional profile information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/account](https://app.getsite.ai/account).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Notification Settings">
    Configure which notifications you want to receive and how.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Ensure you save any changes made to your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subscription Settings">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/account?plan=developer](https://app.getsite.ai/account?plan=developer)
    (or your current plan).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Current Plan">
    See your current subscription details, including pricing and features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Plan">
    Select a different plan if you wish to upgrade or downgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment Method">
    Add or change your payment information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Review and confirm any changes to your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deleting Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Deletion">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/account/delete](https://app.getsite.ai/account/delete).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Implications">
    Read the information about what happens when you delete your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    Get confirmation that your account deletion process has begun.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subscription Plans

GetSite offers several subscription plans to meet different needs:

- **Free Plan**: Basic access with limited features
- **Maker Plan**: For individual creators ($10/month or $84/year)
- **Developer Plan**: For professional developers ($40/month or $336/year)
- **Agency Plan**: For agencies managing multiple clients ($160/month or $1,344/year)

For detailed information about plan features and pricing, see the [Pricing Plans](/pricing/plans) documentation.

## Best Practices

- **Regular Updates**: Keep your profile information current
- **Strong Passwords**: Use secure passwords and consider enabling two-factor authentication
- **Review Billing**: Regularly check your billing statements and credit usage
- **Plan Appropriately**: Choose the subscription plan that best fits your needs
- **Stay Informed**: Keep up with account notifications about important changes
- **Backup Data**: Periodically export important data for your records
