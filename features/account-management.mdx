## Overview

Manage your GetSite account settings, subscription, and preferences from your account page.

## Account Links

- **Settings**: [app.getsite.ai/account](https://app.getsite.ai/account)
- **API Keys**: [app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys](https://app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys)
- **Branding**: [app.getsite.ai/account/branding](https://app.getsite.ai/account/branding)
- **Delete Account**: [app.getsite.ai/account/delete](https://app.getsite.ai/account/delete)
- **Login**: [app.getsite.ai/login](https://app.getsite.ai/login)
- **Register**: [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register)

## Registration and Authentication

- **Email/Password**: Register with email and verify your email address
- **Google Sign-in**: Quick sign-up and login with your Google account (no email verification required)

## Subscription Management

View and change your subscription at [app.getsite.ai/plans](https://app.getsite.ai/plans).

### Plans

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly | Credits/month |
|------|---------|--------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 0 |
| **Maker** | $10/mo | $84/yr ($7/mo) | 100 |
| **Developer** | $40/mo | $336/yr ($28/mo) | 500 |
| **Max** | $160/mo | $1,344/yr ($112/mo) | 2,500 |

- **Upgrades** are prorated immediately.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the next billing cycle.
- **Cancellation**: Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

The Maker plan includes a 5-day free trial on monthly billing with 30 bonus credits.

For detailed plan information, see the [Pricing Plans](/pricing/plans) documentation.

## Branding

Save your brand settings at [app.getsite.ai/account/branding](https://app.getsite.ai/account/branding). See [Branding](/features/branding) for details.

## API Access

Manage API keys at [app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys](https://app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys). Programmatic access to site creation and management. Available on Max plan only.

## Account Deletion

Delete your account at [app.getsite.ai/account/delete](https://app.getsite.ai/account/delete).

## Usage Instructions

### Creating an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration Page">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Registration Method">
    Select either email/password registration or Google authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    If using email registration, verify your email address through the sent
    link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Plans">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/plans](https://app.getsite.ai/plans).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Current Plan">
    See your current subscription details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Plan">
    Select a different plan to upgrade or downgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Review and confirm any changes to your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>
