## Overview

GetSite's website creation and management feature allows you to easily build, customize, and maintain websites through an intuitive interface. This core functionality is designed to make web development accessible to users of all skill levels.

## Key Features

### Site Creation

GetSite offers multiple ways to create a new website:

- **Prompt-Based Creation**: Describe your website in natural language and let AI generate it
- **Chat-Based Creation**: Have a conversation with the AI assistant to build your site
- **Template-Based Creation**: Start with pre-designed templates
- **Upload Existing Sites**: Import and enhance websites you already have

For detailed instructions, see the [How to Create a Website](/create-website) guide.

### Site Settings

Once your website is created, you can configure various settings to customize its behavior:

- **General Settings**: Configure basic website information
- **SEO Settings**: Optimize your site for search engines
- **Performance Settings**: Improve your website's loading speed
- **Privacy Settings**: Configure cookie notices and privacy features
- **Analytics Integration**: Connect with analytics tools

### Site Editing

The built-in [Website Editor](/features/website-editor) provides a comprehensive environment for customizing your website:

- **Visual Editing**: Drag-and-drop interface for easy modifications
- **Code Editing**: Direct HTML, CSS, and JavaScript editing for advanced users
- **Content Management**: Easy text and media content updates
- **Layout Adjustments**: Modify the structure of your pages

### Dashboard

The [Dashboard](https://app.getsite.ai/dashboard) provides an overview of all your websites and their performance:

- **Website Listings**: View all your created websites in one place
- **Performance Metrics**: Track visitors, engagement, and other key metrics
- **Status Indicators**: Monitor the health and status of your websites
- **Quick Actions**: Access common actions directly from the dashboard

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/dashboard](https://app.getsite.ai/dashboard) to
    view all your websites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Website">
    Click on the "Create New Site" button or navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai) to start the creation process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Existing Websites">
    Click on any website in your dashboard to access its management options: -
    **Edit**: Open the website editor - **Settings**: Configure website settings
    - **Analytics**: View performance data - **Delete**: Remove the website
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Changes">
    After making changes to your website, click the "Publish" button to make
    them live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

- **Regular Updates**: Keep your website content fresh and up-to-date
- **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure your website looks good on all devices
- **Performance Monitoring**: Regularly check analytics to understand user behavior
- **Backup**: Create regular backups of your website configuration
- **SEO Management**: Continuously optimize your site for search engines
