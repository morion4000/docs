## Overview

Save your brand settings to automatically apply them when generating new sites. Define your brand's visual identity, voice, and online presence in one place.

Manage your brand settings at [app.getsite.ai/account/branding](https://app.getsite.ai/account/branding).

## Brand Settings

Store the following brand elements:

- **Brand Colors**: Define your primary and secondary color palette
- **Fonts**: Set your preferred typography
- **Logo**: Upload your brand logo
- **Favicon**: Set your site favicon
- **Tagline**: Define your brand tagline
- **Social Links**: Add links to your social media profiles
- **Voice**: Describe your brand's tone and communication style

## How It Works

When you generate a new site, your saved brand settings are automatically applied. This ensures consistency across all your websites without having to specify brand details each time.

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Branding Settings">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/account/branding](https://app.getsite.ai/account/branding).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Brand Elements">
    Fill in your brand colors, fonts, logo, favicon, tagline, social links, and voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Save your brand settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Sites">
    When you create new sites, your brand settings will be automatically applied.
  </Step>
</Steps>
