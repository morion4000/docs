## Overview

Integrate GetSite with 5,000+ apps through Zapier. Trigger site creation from external events and automate your workflows. Available on Developer plan and above.

Manage automations at [app.getsite.ai/automations](https://app.getsite.ai/automations).

## Key Features

### Zapier Integration

Connect GetSite with thousands of other applications through Zapier:

- **Trigger site creation** from external events
- **Bi-directional data flow** between GetSite and other apps
- **No-code setup** with Zapier's visual builder
- **Pre-built templates** for common workflows

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Automations">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/automations](https://app.getsite.ai/automations).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect with Zapier">
    Click on "Connect with Zapier" and follow the authentication process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Zap">
    Set up triggers and actions to automate your workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Activate">
    Test your automation and activate it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Domain Limits

Automation domain limits apply per plan:

| Plan | Domain Limit |
|------|-------------|
| Maker | 10 |
| Developer | 50 |
| Max | Unlimited |

## Plan Availability

Automations are available on the **Developer** plan and above.
