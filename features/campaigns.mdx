Send email campaigns directly from GetSite. Available on Developer plan and above.

Manage at [app.getsite.ai/campaigns](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns).

## How it works

1. **Create a mailing list** at [/campaigns/lists](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/lists) — upload recipients via CSV.
2. **Add a sender address** at [/campaigns/senders](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/senders) — verify before sending.
3. **Create an email template** at [/campaigns/emails](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/emails).
4. **Send a campaign** — select your list, pick a template, send.

Bounce and complaint tracking included. Costs 1 credit per 10 emails sent.
