## Settings

Manage your account at [app.getsite.ai/account](https://app.getsite.ai/account).

Sign up with email or Google at [app.getsite.ai/register](https://app.getsite.ai/register). Email signup requires verification; Google does not.

## Branding

Save brand colors, fonts, logo, favicon, tagline, social links, and voice at [app.getsite.ai/account/branding](https://app.getsite.ai/account/branding). These are automatically applied when you generate new sites.

## API Access

Manage API keys at [app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys](https://app.getsite.ai/account/api-keys). Programmatic access to site creation and management. Max plan only.

## Subscription

View and change plans at [app.getsite.ai/plans](https://app.getsite.ai/plans). Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Cancel anytime — access continues until the end of the current period.

## Delete Account

[app.getsite.ai/account/delete](https://app.getsite.ai/account/delete)
