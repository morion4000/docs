## Overview

Run experiments to optimize your websites with A/B testing. Create experiments with multiple site variants, distribute traffic with custom weights, and track conversion events to find the best-performing version. Available on Max plan only.

Manage your experiments at [app.getsite.ai/experiments](https://app.getsite.ai/experiments).

## Key Features

### Experiment Creation

Set up experiments to compare different versions of your website:

- **Multiple Variants**: Test multiple site variants against each other
- **Traffic Distribution**: Configure weighted traffic distribution across variants
- **Conversion Tracking**: Track conversion events from your hosted site

### Experiment Lifecycle

Manage experiments through their full lifecycle:

- **Draft**: Configure your experiment before launching
- **Running**: Experiment is live and collecting data
- **Paused**: Temporarily stop an experiment
- **Completed**: Experiment is finished with results

### Automatic Winner Detection

The system automatically detects the winning variant based on conversion data.

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Experiment">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/experiments/create](https://app.getsite.ai/experiments/create) and set up a new experiment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Site Variants">
    Select the site variants you want to test and configure traffic distribution weights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Conversion Events">
    Define what counts as a conversion event on your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Experiment">
    Start the experiment to begin distributing traffic to your variants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Results">
    Track results at [app.getsite.ai/experiments](https://app.getsite.ai/experiments) and view winner detection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Site Settings Integration

A/B tests assigned to a site can also be managed from the site's settings page under the **A/B Tests** tab.

## Plan Availability

A/B Testing is available on the **Max** plan only.
