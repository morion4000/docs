Accept payments on your websites through Stripe. Available on Developer plan and above.

Manage at [app.getsite.ai/payments](https://app.getsite.ai/payments).

## Setup

1. Go to [app.getsite.ai/payments](https://app.getsite.ai/payments).
2. Connect your Stripe account with a restricted API key.
3. Create payment links for products, services, or donations.
4. Add payment buttons to your websites.

## What's supported

- Credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods
- One-time payments, subscriptions, and donations
- PCI compliant — customer payment data never touches GetSite servers
