## Overview

Manage your files and AI-generated content at [app.getsite.ai/assets](https://app.getsite.ai/assets). Upload your own files, browse AI-generated images, and manage website design mockups.

## Asset Categories

### Uploads

Upload and manage your own files:

- **Drag-and-Drop**: Simple interface for uploading files
- **Bulk Upload**: Upload multiple files simultaneously
- **File Organization**: Organize your uploads

### AI Images

Browse and manage images generated with Flux AI models:

- **Standard Quality**: 1 credit per image
- **High Quality**: 2 credits per image
- All AI-generated images are royalty-free and commercially usable
- Stock photo search via Unsplash is also available

### AI Designs

Browse and manage website design mockups generated with AI:

- **Design Mockups**: Generate website design mockups from a prompt (5 credits)
- **Browse Designs**: View all generated designs at [app.getsite.ai/designs](https://app.getsite.ai/designs)
- **Create New Design**: Generate a new design at [app.getsite.ai/design](https://app.getsite.ai/design)

## Usage Instructions

### Uploading Files

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Asset Management">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/assets](https://app.getsite.ai/assets).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Files">
    Click the "Upload" button or drag and drop files into the designated area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use in Websites">
    Select assets to use in your website content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Generating AI Images

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Images">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/assets](https://app.getsite.ai/assets) and select the AI Images tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate New Images">
    Describe what you want and generate AI images (requires credits).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use in Websites">
    Select AI-generated images to use in your website content.
  </Step>
</Steps>
