---
title: Forms & Leads
description: "Contact forms, submissions, and lead collection"
---

Built-in contact form handling for all websites. Available on Maker plan and above.

- **Forms**: [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms) — manage forms and view submissions
- **Leads**: [app.getsite.ai/leads](https://app.getsite.ai/leads) — view and export collected leads

Email notifications on new submissions. Google reCAPTCHA spam protection. Email validation on submissions.

You can also view submissions for a specific site from its **Forms** tab in site settings.
