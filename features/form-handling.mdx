## Overview

GetSite provides built-in contact form handling for all websites. Collect form submissions, receive email notifications, and manage leads — all from your dashboard.

Manage forms at [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms). View collected leads at [app.getsite.ai/leads](https://app.getsite.ai/leads).

## Key Features

### Contact Form Handling

- Built-in contact form handling for all websites
- Email notifications on new submissions
- Google reCAPTCHA spam protection
- Email validation on submissions

### Lead Management

- View and export collected leads
- Manage form submissions from the dashboard

## Usage Instructions

### Managing Forms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Submissions">
    Click on a form to view all submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Data">
    Export submission data as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Leads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Leads">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/leads](https://app.getsite.ai/leads).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Leads">
    View all leads collected from your forms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Leads">
    Export leads for use in other systems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Site-Level Form Submissions

You can also view form submissions for a specific site from the site's settings page under the **Forms** tab.

## Plan Availability

Contact forms and leads are available on the **Maker** plan and above.
