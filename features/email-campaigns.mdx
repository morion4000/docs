## Overview

Send email marketing campaigns directly from GetSite. Create mailing lists, design email templates, and send campaigns with bounce and complaint tracking. Available on Developer plan and above.

Manage your campaigns at [app.getsite.ai/campaigns](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns).

## Key Features

### Mailing Lists

Create and manage subscriber lists for your campaigns:

- **Create Lists**: Organize recipients into targeted mailing lists
- **CSV Upload**: Upload recipients in bulk via CSV files
- **List Management**: Add, remove, and manage subscribers

Manage your lists at [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/lists](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/lists).

### Sender Email Addresses

Configure verified sender addresses for your campaigns:

- **Add Senders**: Register email addresses to send campaigns from
- **Verification**: Verify sender addresses before use

Manage senders at [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/senders](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/senders).

### Email Templates

Design professional email templates for your campaigns:

- **Template Editor**: Create and customize email templates
- **Reusable Templates**: Save templates for future campaigns

Manage templates at [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/emails](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/emails).

### Campaign Tracking

Monitor the performance of your email campaigns:

- **Bounce Tracking**: Track bounced emails
- **Complaint Tracking**: Monitor spam complaints

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Mailing List">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/lists](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/lists) and create a new list. Upload recipients via CSV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Sender Address">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/senders](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/senders) and add a verified sender email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an Email Template">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/campaigns/emails](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns/emails) and design your email template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Send Campaign">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/campaigns](https://app.getsite.ai/campaigns), create a new campaign, select your mailing list and template, then send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credit Cost

Sending campaigns costs 1 credit per 10 emails sent.

For more information about credits, see the [Credits System](/pricing/credits) documentation.

## Plan Availability

Email Campaigns is available on the **Developer** plan and above.
