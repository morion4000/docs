## Overview

GetSite's AI Chat at [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat) provides an interactive interface for building websites through conversation. Describe what you want, refine your ideas, and generate a complete website through natural dialogue.

## Key Features

### Chat Interface

- **Conversational AI**: Communicate in natural language
- **Context Awareness**: AI remembers previous interactions in the conversation
- **Multi-turn Dialogue**: Build upon previous questions and responses
- **Rich Formatting**: Support for code snippets, links, and formatted text
- **File Uploads**: Share images or files directly in the chat

### Website Creation via Chat

Use the AI chat as an alternative to prompt-based creation:

- Describe your website goals and the AI guides you through the process
- Provide details about design, content, and features through conversation
- The AI generates a complete website based on your conversation
- Refine and iterate through continued dialogue

## Usage Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Chat">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Conversation">
    Begin by describing the website you want to create or ask for help with an existing site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Share information about your goals, design preferences, and content needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    The AI creates your website based on the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine">
    Continue the conversation to request changes and improvements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: Provide clear details about what you need
- **Start Simple**: Begin with your main goal before diving into details
- **Provide Feedback**: Let the AI know if its suggestions match your vision
- **Follow Up**: Continue the conversation to refine results
