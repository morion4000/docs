## Prompt-Based Creation (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Website Creation">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai) and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Website">
    In the prompt field, describe what kind of website you want to create. Be as specific as possible about:
    - Purpose of the website
    - Design preferences
    - Content sections needed
    - Color schemes
    - Any specific features

    **Example**: "Create a professional portfolio website for a photographer with a dark theme, gallery section, about me page, and contact form."

  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Click the "Create" button and wait while GetSite generates your website based on your description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Edit">
    Once generated, you'll be able to preview your website. Use the [editor](/features/website-editor) to make any necessary adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Website">
    When you're satisfied with your website, click the "Publish" button to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Chat-Based Creation

If you prefer a more conversational approach:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Chat">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat) and log in to
    your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Conversation">
    Begin chatting with the AI assistant about the website you want to create.
    You can ask questions, provide details, and refine your ideas through
    conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Once you've discussed your requirements, the AI will help you generate a
    website based on your conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Edit">
    Review the generated website and make any necessary adjustments using the
    editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Website">
    When you're satisfied with your website, publish it to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload an Existing Website

If you already have a website that you want to enhance or manage through GetSite:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Upload Feature">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/upload](https://app.getsite.ai/upload) and log
    in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Website Files">
    Upload your website files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images, etc.) following
    the on-screen instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up any additional configurations for your uploaded website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enhance with GetSite Features">
    Utilize GetSite's features to enhance your uploaded website, such as adding
    forms, optimizing images, or improving SEO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Website">
    When you're satisfied with your enhanced website, publish it to make it
    live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Website Creation Tips

- **Be specific** in your prompts or conversations with the AI
- **Use keywords** that describe your desired style, features, and content
- **Review the preview** carefully before publishing
- **Make use of the editor** to fine-tune your website
- **Consider your audience** when designing your website
