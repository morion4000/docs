## Overview

GetSite integrates with third-party services through custom scripts and automation platforms.

## Custom Scripts

Add third-party scripts to your website through the **Scripts** tab in site settings:

- **Head section** — Analytics, tracking pixels, fonts
- **Body end** — Chat widgets, marketing tools, payment buttons

### Supported Services

Supports any JavaScript-based service, including:

- **Google Analytics** — Website analytics
- **Google Tag Manager** — Tag management
- **Facebook Pixel** — Ad tracking
- **HotJar** — Heatmaps and recordings
- **Intercom** — Live chat
- **Crisp** — Live chat
- **Mailchimp** — Email signup forms
- Any custom JavaScript

## Analytics Integration

### Supported Analytics Tools

- **Google Analytics**: Comprehensive website analytics
- **Google Search Console**: Monitor search engine performance
- **Google Tag Manager**: Manage and deploy website tags
- **Plausible**: Simple and privacy-friendly analytics

### Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Site Settings">
    From your dashboard, select the website and navigate to Settings > Scripts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tracking Code">
    Paste your tracking code or pixel into the head or body section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Publish">
    Save and publish your site to activate the tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>
