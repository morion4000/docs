## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is GetSite.ai?">
    An AI-powered website builder that creates fully designed, hosted websites
    from a text prompt. No coding or design skills needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
    There is a free tier with 1 site. Paid plans: Maker ($10/mo), Developer
    ($40/mo), Max ($160/mo). Yearly billing saves 30%. See the full
    [pricing page](https://www.getsite.ai/pricing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. The Maker plan (monthly billing) includes a 5-day free trial with 30
    bonus credits. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are credits?">
    Credits are used for AI-powered actions like creating sites, editing, and
    generating images. Each plan includes a monthly credit allowance. Additional
    credits can be purchased separately. See the [Credits System](/pricing/credits)
    for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over?">
    No. Monthly credits reset each billing period. Purchased add-on credits
    never expire.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Yes. Paid plans support custom domains (Maker: 10, Developer: 50, Max:
    Unlimited). You can buy, transfer, or link an existing domain. See
    [Domain Management](/features/domain-management) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need my own hosting?">
    No. All paid plans include managed hosting with CDN and free SSL
    certificates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my site?">
    Yes. Download your website as a ZIP file with all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
    Available on Maker plan and above, or $10 one-time fee per site.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it mobile-friendly?">
    Yes. All generated sites are fully responsive by default.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add custom code?">
    Yes. Add custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in the head or body of your site
    through the Scripts tab in site settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do upgrades and downgrades work?">
    Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next
    billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Website Creation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a website?">
    There are three ways to create a website:
    1. **Prompt** — Describe what you want at [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai)
    2. **AI Chat** — Build through conversation at [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat)
    3. **Clone & Remix** — Enter any URL and the AI creates a version inspired by it

    For detailed instructions, see our [How to Create a Website](/create-website) guide.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take?">
    The AI generates a complete website in under 60 seconds. Additional time may
    be needed for customization and refinement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit my website after it's created?">
    Yes. Use the AI editor to modify text, images, sections, and layouts. You
    can also use the full code editor on the Developer plan and above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own images?">
    Yes. Upload your own images through Asset Management, or generate custom
    AI images with Flux models. Stock photo search via Unsplash is also
    available.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Domains and Publishing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to purchase a domain?">
    No. GetSite offers free subdomains on getsite.online and getsite.co. For a
    more professional appearance, you can buy, transfer, or link a custom
    domain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I connect my own domain?">
    You can:
    1. **Buy a domain** through GetSite at [/domains/buy](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/buy)
    2. **Transfer** an existing domain at [/domains/transfer](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/transfer)
    3. **Link** an external domain via CNAME at [/domains/link](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/link)

    See our [Domain Management](/features/domain-management) documentation for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does GetSite provide SSL certificates?">
    Yes. All paid plans include free SSL certificates automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does GetSite support SEO?">
    Yes. Custom page titles, meta descriptions, Open Graph tags, automatic XML
    sitemaps, and robots.txt. All sites are responsive by default (a key
    ranking factor).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
