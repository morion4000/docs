## Free Subdomain Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Free Subdomain">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/domains/get](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/get).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Subdomain Name">
    Enter your preferred subdomain prefix (e.g., "mybusiness" for
    mybusiness.getsite.online).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Availability">
    Verify that your chosen subdomain is available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to Website">
    Select which website you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Website">
    Visit your new subdomain to ensure your website loads correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Buy a Domain Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Domain Purchase">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/domains/buy](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/buy).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Domain">
    Enter your desired domain name in the search box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Domain">
    Choose from available TLDs and review pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase">
    Enter registration information and complete payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to Website">
    Select which website to connect to this domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Propagation">
    Allow 24-48 hours for DNS propagation to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Visit your new domain to verify that your website loads correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transfer a Domain Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Domain Transfer">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/domains/transfer](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/transfer).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Domain Information">
    Provide the domain name you wish to transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change NS Records">
    Change the NS records at your current registrar to the ones provided by GetSite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Transfer">
    Domain transfers typically take 5-7 days to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Once transferred, configure domain settings including email aliases, DNS records, and uptime monitoring.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Domain Settings

Once your domain is set up, manage it from its settings page at [app.getsite.ai/domains/:domainId](https://app.getsite.ai/domains/:domainId):

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Links** | Assign sites to paths on the domain |
| **Redirects** | URL redirect rules (301 redirects) |
| **Emails** | Email alias forwarding (transferred domains only) |
| **Analytics** | Domain-level traffic analytics |
| **Uptime** | Uptime monitoring with email alerts |
| **DNS** | DNS record management |
| **Settings** | General domain configuration |

## Best Practices

### Domain Selection

- **Keep it short** and memorable
- **Avoid numbers and hyphens** if possible
- **Consider multiple TLDs** for brand protection

### DNS Management

- **Set reasonably low TTL values** during initial setup (300-900 seconds)
- **Increase TTL values** after configuration is stable (3600+ seconds)

### Security

- **Enable domain privacy** to hide personal information
- **Use strong passwords** on your registrar account
- **Enable two-factor authentication** where available
- **Lock your domain** to prevent unauthorized transfers
- **Set up auto-renewal** to prevent accidental expiration
