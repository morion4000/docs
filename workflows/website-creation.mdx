## Overview

The GetSite website creation workflow provides a streamlined process for designing, building, and publishing websites. This guide walks you through the entire journey from initial concept to publishing your finished website.

## Complete Website Creation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan Your Website">
    Before starting, define your website's: - Purpose and goals - Target
    audience - Key content sections - Design preferences - Functionality
    requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Creation Method">
    Select the approach that works best for you: - Prompt-based creation
    (recommended for most users) - AI chat-based creation - Template-based
    creation - Upload an existing website
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Initial Website">
    Create your website using your chosen method: - For prompt-based: Write a
    detailed prompt describing your website - For chat-based: Have a
    conversation with the AI about your needs - For template-based: Select and
    customize a template - For upload: Import your existing website files
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Edit">
    Examine the generated website and make necessary adjustments: - Check
    content accuracy and relevance - Verify design elements and layout - Test
    functionality and links - Add or modify sections as needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Media and Assets">
    Enhance your website with visual and media elements: - Upload or generate
    images - Add videos if needed - Include documents or downloadable files -
    Create or update logos and brand elements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Forms">
    Set up forms to collect information from visitors: - Create contact forms -
    Set up newsletter sign-ups - Configure form notifications - Implement spam
    protection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize for SEO">
    Improve your website's search engine visibility: - Set page titles and meta
    descriptions - Optimize headings and content structure - Configure Open
    Graph tags for social sharing - Create a sitemap
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mobile Optimization">
    Ensure your website works well on all devices: - Test responsive design -
    Verify readability on small screens - Check touch-friendly navigation -
    Optimize images for mobile loading
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Domain">
    Set up a domain for your website: - Use a free GetSite subdomain - Purchase
    a new domain - Connect an existing domain - Configure DNS settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Test">
    Perform final checks before publishing: - Test all links and functionality -
    Verify forms work correctly - Check spelling and grammar - Test loading
    speed - Preview on multiple devices and browsers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Website">
    Make your website live: - Click the "Publish" button - Confirm publishing
    options - Wait for confirmation that your site is live
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post-Launch Tasks">
    After publishing, complete these important tasks: - Share your website on
    social media - Set up analytics to track visitors - Create a backup of your
    website - Plan for regular content updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompt-Based Creation Method

The recommended approach for most users is the prompt-based creation method:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Website Creation">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai) and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Website">
    Click on the "Create New Site" button from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Detailed Prompt">
    In the prompt field, describe your website in detail. Include information about:
    - Website purpose (e.g., portfolio, business, blog)
    - Design style (e.g., minimalist, colorful, professional)
    - Key sections needed (e.g., about, services, contact)
    - Color preferences (if any)
    - Specific functionality requirements

    **Example Prompt**: "Create a professional portfolio website for a photographer specializing in nature photography. The site should have a dark theme that makes the photos stand out. Include sections for portfolio gallery, about me, services, testimonials, and contact. The design should be elegant and minimalist with easy navigation."

  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Click the "Create" button and wait while GetSite generates your website based on your description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Generated Website">
    Once generated, examine the website and note any adjustments needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and Refine">
    Use the editor to make necessary changes to the generated website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue with Standard Process">
    Proceed with steps 5-12 from the complete workflow above to finalize and publish your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Chat-Based Creation Method

If you prefer a more conversational approach to website creation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Chat">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat) and log in to
    your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Conversation">
    Begin by telling the AI about the website you want to create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details Through Conversation">
    Through natural conversation, provide details about: - Your website goals -
    Design preferences - Content needs - Specific features The AI will ask
    clarifying questions to better understand your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review AI Suggestions">
    The AI will offer suggestions and ideas for your website. Provide feedback
    on these suggestions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    When you're satisfied with the plan, ask the AI to generate your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Edit">
    Use the editor to make any necessary adjustments to the generated website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue with Standard Process">
    Proceed with steps 5-12 from the complete workflow to finalize and publish
    your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

### Effective Prompts

To get the best results from prompt-based creation:

- **Be specific** about your website's purpose and target audience
- **Describe the visual style** you want, including colors and design elements
- **List all needed sections** in order of importance
- **Mention specific functionality** requirements (forms, galleries, etc.)
- **Include industry context** to help the AI generate relevant content

### Efficient Editing

To streamline the editing process:

- **Address structural issues first** before fine-tuning content
- **Use section editing** to focus on one area at a time
- **Save regularly** to avoid losing changes
- **Test progressively** rather than all at once at the end
- **Use AI assistance** for content generation and improvements

### Pre-Launch Checklist

Before publishing, verify these essential elements:

- ✅ All links work correctly
- ✅ Contact information is accurate
- ✅ Forms submit properly and notifications work
- ✅ Images load quickly and display correctly
- ✅ Content is free of spelling and grammar errors
- ✅ Website displays correctly on mobile devices
- ✅ SEO elements are properly configured
- ✅ Legal pages (privacy policy, terms) are included if needed
