## Overview

This guide walks you through the entire journey from initial concept to publishing your finished website with GetSite.

## Complete Website Creation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan Your Website">
    Before starting, define your website's:
    - Purpose and goals
    - Target audience
    - Key content sections
    - Design preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Creation Method">
    Select the approach that works best for you:
    - **Prompt** ([app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai)) — Describe what you want
    - **AI Chat** ([app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat)) — Build through conversation
    - **Clone & Remix** — Enter any URL to create a version inspired by it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Initial Website">
    The AI builds a complete website with custom copy, images, and a responsive layout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with the AI Editor">
    Use the editor to perfect every detail:
    - Edit text, links, and images by clicking on elements
    - Edit sections or use AI to regenerate them
    - Tell the AI Developer what changes you want in plain language
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure SEO">
    Set page title, meta description, and Open Graph image in the site's SEO settings tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Domain">
    Set up a domain for your website:
    - Use a free subdomain on getsite.online or getsite.co
    - Buy, transfer, or link a custom domain
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Test">
    Test all links, check on mobile devices, and verify forms work correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Website">
    Click "Publish" to make your website live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompt-Based Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Website Creation">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai](https://app.getsite.ai) and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a Detailed Prompt">
    Describe your website in detail. Include information about:
    - Website purpose (e.g., portfolio, business, landing page)
    - Design style (e.g., minimalist, colorful, professional)
    - Key sections needed (e.g., about, services, contact)
    - Color preferences (if any)

    **Example**: "Create a professional portfolio website for a photographer specializing in nature photography. The site should have a dark theme with sections for portfolio gallery, about me, services, testimonials, and contact."

  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Website">
    Click "Create" and wait while GetSite generates your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine and Publish">
    Use the AI editor to make adjustments, then publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Chat-Based Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Chat">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/chat](https://app.getsite.ai/chat).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Conversation">
    Tell the AI about the website you want to create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Share your goals, design preferences, and content needs through conversation. The AI will ask clarifying questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and Refine">
    The AI generates your website. Continue the conversation to request changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Publish when you're satisfied with the result.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

### Effective Prompts

- **Be specific** about your website's purpose and target audience
- **Describe the visual style** you want
- **List all needed sections** in order of importance
- **Mention specific functionality** requirements (forms, galleries, etc.)
- **Include industry context** to help the AI generate relevant content

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- All links work correctly
- Contact information is accurate
- Forms submit properly and notifications work
- Images load and display correctly
- Content is free of spelling and grammar errors
- Website displays correctly on mobile devices
- SEO elements are properly configured
