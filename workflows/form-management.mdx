## Overview

The GetSite form management workflow provides a comprehensive process for creating, customizing, and managing forms on your websites. This guide walks you through everything from basic form creation to advanced form management and data collection.

## Form Creation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms) and log in
    to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Form">Click on the "Create a form" button.</Step>
  <Step title="Set up Email Notifications">
    Configure who receives notifications when the form is submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Form">
    Submit test entries to ensure everything works correctly: - Verify all
    fields work as expected - Check validation rules - Confirm notifications are
    sent - Test the success message or redirect
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Form Submissions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Form">
    Click on the form whose submissions you want to view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Submissions List">
    Access the list of all form submissions, showing: - Submission date and time
    - Submitter information - Submission status - Key field values
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter and Search">
    Use filters and search to find specific submissions: - Date range filters -
    Status filters - Keyword search - Field value filters
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Individual Submissions">
    Click on any submission to view its complete details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Submission Status">
    Update submission status for tracking purposes: - New - In Progress -
    Completed - Flagged - Archived
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Submission Data">
    Export submission data in various formats: - CSV - Excel - PDF - JSON
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Collected Emails

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Collected Emails">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/forms/emails](https://app.getsite.ai/forms/emails).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Email List">
    Access the consolidated list of all emails collected across your forms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Email List">
    Export your email list for use in other systems: - CSV format - Excel format
    - Email service provider format
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Validation">
    Verify email validity: - Check for format errors - Identify potential fake
    emails - Mark verified emails
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Editing and Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Form to Edit">
    Click on the "Edit" button next to the form you want to modify.
  </Step>
</Steps>
