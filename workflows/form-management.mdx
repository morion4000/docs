## Overview

The GetSite form management workflow provides a comprehensive process for creating, customizing, and managing forms on your websites. This guide walks you through everything from basic form creation to advanced form management and data collection.

## Form Creation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms) and log in
    to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Form">Click on the "Create New Form" button.</Step>
  <Step title="Select Form Type">
    Choose from available form types: - Contact Form - Newsletter Signup -
    Feedback Form - Survey Form - Registration Form - Custom Form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basic Settings">
    Set up fundamental form attributes: - Form name (internal reference) - Form
    title (displayed to users) - Success message (shown after submission) -
    Redirect URL (optional destination after submission)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Form Fields">
    Add and configure the fields needed for your form: - Text fields (short and
    long) - Email field - Phone number - Name fields - Dropdown menus -
    Checkboxes - Radio buttons - File uploads - Date pickers - Hidden fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Field Properties">
    For each field, configure: - Label text - Placeholder text - Required status
    - Validation rules - Default values - Help text
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize Fields">
    Arrange your form fields in a logical order: - Drag and drop to reposition -
    Group related fields - Add section headers - Create multi-step forms if
    needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Email Notifications">
    Configure who receives notifications when the form is submitted: - Admin
    notification email(s) - Notification subject line - Email template
    customization - Reply-to settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Auto-responders">
    Set up automatic responses to form submitters: - Enable/disable
    auto-response - Subject line - Email content - Attachments (if needed)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Spam Protection">
    Set up protection against spam submissions: - Enable reCAPTCHA - Email
    validation - Honeypot fields - Rate limiting
  </Step>
  <Step title="Style Your Form">
    Customize the form appearance: - Color scheme - Button styles - Field
    styling - Layout options - Mobile optimization
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Get Embed Code">
    Save your form configuration and retrieve the embed code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Form to Website">
    Add the form to your website using one of these methods: - Embed code
    insertion - Form widget selection in the website editor - Dedicated form
    page creation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Form">
    Submit test entries to ensure everything works correctly: - Verify all
    fields work as expected - Check validation rules - Confirm notifications are
    sent - Test the success message or redirect
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Form Submissions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Form">
    Click on the form whose submissions you want to view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Submissions List">
    Access the list of all form submissions, showing: - Submission date and time
    - Submitter information - Submission status - Key field values
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter and Search">
    Use filters and search to find specific submissions: - Date range filters -
    Status filters - Keyword search - Field value filters
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Individual Submissions">
    Click on any submission to view its complete details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Submission Status">
    Update submission status for tracking purposes: - New - In Progress -
    Completed - Flagged - Archived
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Internal Notes">
    Attach notes to submissions for team communication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Submission Data">
    Export submission data in various formats: - CSV - Excel - PDF - JSON
  </Step>
  <Step title="Batch Operations">
    Perform actions on multiple submissions: - Bulk status updates - Batch
    deletion - Mass export
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Collected Emails

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Collected Emails">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/forms/emails](https://app.getsite.ai/forms/emails).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Email List">
    Access the consolidated list of all emails collected across your forms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter and Organize">
    Use filters to organize your email list: - Form source - Submission date -
    Subscription status - Custom tags
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag Management">
    Apply tags to categorize email addresses: - Create new tags - Apply tags to
    selected emails - Filter by tags
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Email List">
    Export your email list for use in other systems: - CSV format - Excel format
    - Email service provider format
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Subscriptions">
    Handle subscription status: - View opt-in status - Process unsubscribe
    requests - Update preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Validation">
    Verify email validity: - Check for format errors - Identify potential fake
    emails - Mark verified emails
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Editing and Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Form to Edit">
    Click on the "Edit" button next to the form you want to modify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Form Configuration">
    Make necessary changes to your form: - Add or remove fields - Update field
    properties - Change notification settings - Adjust styling options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">Save your updated form configuration.</Step>
  <Step title="Update Form on Website">
    If you've made significant changes: - Re-embed the form if needed - Update
    any custom styling - Check mobile responsiveness
  </Step>
  <Step title="Form Analytics Review">
    Analyze form performance: - Submission rates - Completion rates -
    Abandonment points - Field error frequencies
  </Step>
  <Step title="A/B Testing">
    If available, set up A/B tests to optimize form performance: - Create form
    variations - Set test parameters - Monitor performance metrics - Implement
    winning designs
  </Step>
</Steps>

  </Step>

  </Step>

## Form Types and Use Cases

### Contact Form

Ideal for general inquiries and communication:

- Name field
- Email field
- Subject field
- Message field
- Optional phone number
- Consent checkbox

### Newsletter Signup

Streamlined for email collection:

- Email field (required)
- Name field (optional)
- Interest checkboxes
- GDPR/consent checkbox
- Clear privacy information

### Feedback Form

Designed to collect opinions and ratings:

- Rating scales
- Multiple-choice questions
- Open-ended feedback field
- Contact information (optional)
- Anonymous submission option

### Survey Form

Comprehensive for detailed information gathering:

- Multiple sections
- Various question types
- Logic branching
- Progress indicator
- Save and resume functionality

### Application Form

Structured for detailed applications:

- Personal information section
- Qualification fields
- Experience details
- Document uploads
- Reference information
- Terms acceptance

## Best Practices

### Form Design

For optimal user experience:

- **Keep forms short** - only ask for essential information
- **Group related fields** logically
- **Use clear labels** and helpful placeholder text
- **Mark optional fields** rather than required ones
- **Implement inline validation** to catch errors early
- **Make buttons clear and prominent**
- **Ensure mobile responsiveness**

### Data Collection

For responsible data handling:

- **Include clear privacy notices** about how data will be used
- **Only collect data you need** and will use
- **Secure sensitive information** appropriately
- **Set up proper data retention policies**
- **Comply with GDPR** and other relevant regulations
- **Provide easy unsubscribe options** for email lists

### Submission Management

For efficient form processing:

- **Set up clear notification workflows** for your team
- **Establish response time expectations**
- **Create submission handling procedures**
- **Regularly export and back up submission data**
- **Set up automated responses** for common inquiries
- **Use status tracking** to monitor progress
