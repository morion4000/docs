## Overview

This guide walks you through managing forms and leads on your GetSite websites.

## Form Creation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Form">
    Click on the "Create a form" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Email Notifications">
    Configure who receives email notifications when the form is submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Form">
    Submit test entries to ensure everything works correctly:
    - Verify all fields work as expected
    - Confirm notifications are sent
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Form Submissions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Forms Dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.getsite.ai/forms](https://app.getsite.ai/forms).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Form">
    Click on the form whose submissions you want to view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Submissions">
    Browse the list of all form submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Data">
    Export submission data as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also view form submissions for a specific site from the site's settings page under the **Forms** tab.

## Managing Leads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Leads">
    Navigate to
    [app.getsite.ai/leads](https://app.getsite.ai/leads).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Leads">
    Access the consolidated list of all leads collected across your forms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Leads">
    Export your leads for use in other systems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Spam Protection

GetSite provides built-in spam protection for forms:

- **Google reCAPTCHA**: Prevent automated submissions
- **Email Validation**: Verify email addresses on submissions

## Plan Availability

Contact forms and leads are available on the **Maker** plan and above.
